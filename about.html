About Us

- Our Company -

Established in 2020, the ONEIH FARM is the largest agricultural company producer, and it is one of the new and continuously operating company in Nigeria .

Today, ONEIH FARM owns and operates a strategic balance of properties, feedlots and farms comprising around 2.1 thousand hectares of land in Ijakadi and the Northern Territory in OFFA. This equates to roughly 1% of Offa's land mass.

ONEIH FARM specialises in how animals can be bought and treated and also in grain fed beef and Wagyu beef production.

- Leadership -

ONEIH FARM's leaders are skilled and passionate about our Farm. Each are required to hold themselves accountable for contributing to the success of the Farm, to make safety and our people a priority and to work with integrity.

- Our History -

As a world leading producer of agricultural products and Kwara largest beef producer, ONEIH FARM has a long and proud history. We continue to diversify our operations into a vertically integrated agribusiness, backed by a solid history of sustainability and growth.

- Environment and Sustainability -

ONEIH FARM is committed to innovative environmental management which protects the resources on which our production systems are based.

Our environment and sustainability initiatives are driven by a dedicated and skilled team who are committed to best practice and support our people to deliver on environmental compliance.
